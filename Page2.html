智慧政府

掌握即時路況資訊，交通創新再升級

Mastering Real-time Traffic Information, The Latest Upgrade of Traffic Innovation



難得的連續假期，總是既期待出遊又怕卡在車陣裡，浪費大好時光！車上GPS導航雖然提供最佳行駛路線，廣播也隨時播放路況資訊，提醒用路人注意易塞路段。但這些只能讓用路人被動瞭解路況的資訊，那麼，要怎麼樣讓行程更暢行無阻呢？中華電信利用高普及率的eTag，透過訊號與大數據整合分析車輛動線，用路人在行進間若遇到塞車就能主動趕快變換路線，讓遊程更順暢通行。


自從國道五號通車後，大大縮短北宜之間距離，用路人最擔心的頭城交流道路段塞車情形，只要利用路況快易通APP查詢，隨時變換替代道路。遇到雪隧壅塞，馬上轉往台九線、台二線或台二丙線等，瞭解即時車流影像、即時事件交通資訊，透過所提供的各替代道路資訊，相較於以往從頭城塞到南港需耗費2.5小時，依系統建議改走台九線轉坪林交流道至南港幾乎省下一半的時間。而這套便民交通資訊也將延伸至中橫翠峰至大禹嶺路段，以及全國公路網絡，比傳統的偵測系統多了許多資訊。


這項服務利用eTag的大數據資料，可以歸納分析各車輛從哪裡來、花多少時間、在哪些景點停留多少時間等用路人行為，更貼心納入不同季節的熱門景點的交通資訊。透過長期細部數據累積展開，政府單位就能從歷史資料分析，有效規劃交通疏導方式，甚至決策道路是否要拓寬等。智慧城市的應用，提供便民及更有系統整合的交通管理，在尖峰或連假時段進行動態調整，達到順暢省時目的。



另一項智慧管理應用，傳統電視台傳送SNG畫面，都需要事先架設訊號線、發電器材、測試聲音影像等前置作業，中華電信以衛星新聞轉播車為概念的行動SNG APP，利用手機即拍即傳的功能將現場畫面傳送至雲端平台，再加上GPS定位功能等地理資訊，還可運用於災害救援、緊急事件、遠端監控等事項，全方位建構安全的市民生活。


行動SNG以軍規的標準打造各種使用情境，3秒立即啟用上傳，有助於各種災害應變，政府單位能用最快的速度啟動防救災機制。智慧手機普及化的時代已經來臨，小小一支手機即時傳送現場影音資料，將災害防治導入科技及智慧化，可於電腦、平板、手機等載具同步瀏覽影像。2016年7月尼伯特颱風襲臺前，桃園市水務局首度啟用行動SNG作為決策參考依據，連線市府應變中心掌握各項防汛訊息。4G行動寬頻與智慧管理結合的服務，提升政府治理效能，機動協調機關各局處資源調配，讓民眾有感智慧科技的快速效率。



The precious continuous holidays are always filled with expectation of traveling and the fear of getting stuck in the car arrays. Such a waste of good times! Although the GPS navigation in the car can provide the best route at any time and the radio can offer traffic information to remind drivers to pay attention to easily-stuck sections, these can only have drivers passively understand the information. So, how to make the trip smoother? Chunghwa Telecom utilizes eTag with high market share rate and the signal and big data integration analysis of traffic, if drivers encounter traffic jam, they can take the initiative to quickly change the route, so the trip can be smoother.


Ever since the opening of the 5th national highway which significantly shortens the distance between Taipei and Yilan, what drivers worry the most is about the traffic jam of the interchange of Toucheng. By simply using the APP of Quick Traffic, you can find the alternative road at any time. In the event of jams in Hsuehshan Tunnel, you can immediately change the route to Provincial Highway 9, Provincial Highway 2 or Provincial Highway 2c by understanding the real-time traffic images and information. Through the feed of alternative road information, comparing to the past as spending 2.5 hours getting stuck in the section of Touchen-Nangang, taking the reference provided by the system and take the route from PH 9 to Ping Lin Interchange to Nangang which can almost save half of the time. This convenient transportation information will also be extended to the Cuifeng of Central Cross-Island Highway to Dayu Ling, as well as the national highway network, which is better than the traditional detection system as having more information.


This service uses eTag's big data to summarize the information from where the vehicles come from, how much time they spend, how much time they stay in the attractions, and considerably include the traffic information of popular attractions in different seasons. Through the accumulation of long-term details of the data, the government units may be able to analyze from the historical data, effectively plan the traffic guidance, and even make the decision to broaden the road. The application of the smart city can provide the convenience and more systematic integration of traffic management, making dynamic adjustment during periods of peak or continuous holidays to achieve smooth and time-saving purposes.



Another smart management applications is for the traditional television transmission SNG screen which requires the setting up of signal lines, power generation equipment, sound images tests and other pre-operations in advance, The SNG APP of Chunghwa Telecom that utilizes the concept of satellite news car so the transmission of the scene can be captured by smart phones and send the scenes to the cloud platform, coupled with GPS positioning and other geographic information which is able to be used in disaster rescuing, emergency, remote monitoring and other matters, constructing a safe public life for citizens from all aspects.


The mobile SNG uses military standards devices to create a variety of usage scenarios, it only takes 3 seconds to immediately enable the upload which can contribute to a variety of disaster response. Government agencies can use the fastest speed to start disaster relief mechanism. Smart phone widely-populated era has come, a small mobile phone can instantly send live audio and video information that make implement disaster prevention and control technological and smart, people can simultaneously browse the image in computers, tablets, mobile phones and other devices. Before the landing of Typhoon Nepartak in July 2016, Department of Water Resource of Taoyuan City, utilized SNG as a basis for decision-making, connecting the city center response center to master the flood control information for the first time. The combination of service of 4G mobile broadband and smart management can enhance the effectiveness of government governance and those of each mobile coordination agencies bureau of resources deployment, so that people can feel the efficiency of smart technology.
