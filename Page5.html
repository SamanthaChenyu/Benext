智慧旅運

全新航空體驗，行動祕書升級遊程

A New Arial Experience with Trip Upgrade of Mobile Secretary



出國旅遊，繁冗的行程規劃讓人既期待又興奮，最重要的機票事務除了行前訂購之外，出入境通關更是考驗旅人的耐心。國內航空業者推出行動祕書APP，只要輕輕滑動手機就能一手掌握旅行大小事，不用趕著提前到登機櫃台出示印有詳細航程及訂位代號的收據，甚至只要用手機QRcode一掃就能輕鬆通關入境，有別於傳統的紙本登機證流程。


以往出國總是要提前幾個小時到機場，排隊Check in、等出關、找登機門，所有的時間都花費在等待上，甚至還沒閒暇逛免稅商品就準備登機了。上了飛機後有時連餐點都無法選、機上免稅品也賣完了，只能等飛機落地後才開啟真正的放鬆之旅。若是遇到回程班機delay，可能又要焦慮無法聯絡上接機的人，行程中潛在的不確定性大大減低遊興。



航空公司推出專屬的貼心旅遊秘書APP，不只提供航班資訊查詢服務，可依照個人行程適時適地掌握最新航班動態，容享受旅程的每一刻。出發前，查詢行程訂位購票，起飛前1.5至24小時使用預辦登機，節省排隊等候時間。想要提前預訂免稅品，只要於航班起飛前16小時以上(各機場不同)上線訂購，就不用擔心機上免稅品售罄。另外還可以預選餐點，在機上小憩或體驗影音娛樂時，空服員直接將餐點放置桌上，不會再被打擾詢問用餐內容。


結合大數據科技創新應用，個人化互動情境式服務APP跨領域整合4G服務，遊程中若是航班資訊變動，如delay或臨時變更，系統也會同步通知預先設入的聯絡人群組，讓家人安心或接機者隨時掌握動態時程。針對現在旅客或商務人士，每個人都擁有數張電子票券，行動服務APP可整合行程或優惠相關票券方便收納管理，不用再費心整理紙本或擔心遺漏。甚至不須托運行李的旅客，只要利用智慧型手機就能快速通關、出境，結合雲端大數據展開新旅遊型態。


這些便民的貼心服務，讓搭機多了不同體驗，並促進產業整合升級，航空公司在技術上引領創新，積極為轉型物聯網生活服務建置者。藉由發展智慧旅運應用，提供便捷快速服務，節省排隊等候時間，並依個人行程適時適地提供航班資訊及服務，應用服務能帶給大眾更多的便利、節能等效益。



Traveling abroad, a procedure involves complicated but exciting preparation and most of all the bothering pre-booking of plane tickets and patience-testing departure and arrival process. The domestic airlines has introduced mobile secretary APP which no longer requires submission of receipts printed with detail travel routes and booking numbers at the counters, but only a swift of surfing on the mobile phones and all the task of traveling preparation can be completed. Even more, by scanning the QR code can a tourist pass the boarding gates easily, which is quite different from conventional procedure of paper copy boarding pass.


In the past, traveling abroad requires arriving at the airport ahead of several hours, checking in in lines, waiting for clearance, and finding the boarding gate, all the times are spent on waiting, not even having extra time remaining for window shopping duty-free shops before boarding. And there is no selection of meals on the planes and all duty-free goods on aboard are sold out. The trip of relaxation can only begin as soon as the plane lands. However, if the plane of return is delayed, anxiety of unable to contact the pickers would be raised, all these potential uncertainties would decrease the joy of traveling drastically.



The travel secretary APP introduced by the airlines not only provide searching service for flight information, but also available for accessing the status of latest flights based on personal traveling schedule for allowing enjoying the trip. Before departing, it can book flight tickets based on schedules and check in 1.5 to 24 hours before taking off to save times of waiting in lines. If you want to pre-order duty-free goods, all you need to do is ordering online 16 hours or more (depends on each airport) before taking off, and not to worry about they might be sold out. In addition, pre-ordering meals is available. You will be deliver your meal in front of you by the flight attendant without being bothered for asking the selection on the plane while you are taking rests, watching videos or listening to music.


By combining big data technology innovative applications, personalized interaction situational service APP interdisciplinary integrating 4G service. If there is any adjustment of flight information such as delay or temporary change, the system will simultaneously inform the pre-set contact list, making the family not worried or the pickers can access the temporary status of flights. As for the tourists or businessmen recently, each of them has several e-tickets, the mobile service APP can integrate the schedule or related coupons for the convenience of management, not to worry about missing the paper copies while piling up. Even more, for tourists with no needs of check-in baggage, they can make fast clearance, departure as long as they have mobile phones, and combine cloud big data to develop a new traveling type.


All these considerate services for people can make more different experience when boarding, and improve industrial integration upgrade. The airlines play as the establisher of positive transformation toward IoT life service and leading role on innovation. By developing smart travel application to provide faster and more convenient service to save the waiting times in lines and properly provide flight information and service based on personal schedules, and more benefits as convenience or efficiency could be brought out to the mass by applying such service.
