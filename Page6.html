智慧交通

智慧運輸 緊扣城市商圈脈動

Smart Transportation that Tied with Urban Shopping Districts



站在公車候車亭，瞇著眼睛尋找公車路線圖，這樣的生活方式已經過時了！國內電信業者於臺北市重要幹線設立智慧站牌，超大的LCD螢幕清晰顯示站名、公車即時動態到站資訊，天氣資訊及跑馬燈，並提供清晰明瞭的公車路線圖，方便旅客獲取到站資訊。民眾候車期間，除了可使用智慧站牌提供的Gt 4G Wifi上網瀏覽資訊，還可透過智慧LCD站牌觀賞與所在位置附近有關的廣告影片推播與輪播，了解站點周邊推薦店家及優惠資訊。為了掃除治安死角，智慧站牌上方設置了360º魚眼監控鏡頭，可24小時記錄候車亭周邊景象，不只能掌握車輛動態，也能確保乘客的候車安全。


隨著公車服務品質的提升，現在的智慧公車更是搭上最新流行的4G應用，讓運輸服務更有智慧，服務相當貼心。除了有抓寶可夢必備的Gt 4G Wifi免費供乘客使用，且公車所搭載的車載機可回傳車輛位置，協助掌握車況與行駛路線，確保車輛行車安全。碰到惡司機緊急煞車，緊急加減速等惡劣行為，也逃不過智慧公車的法眼。4G智慧公車能夠詳實記錄駕駛的行為、偵測車門未停妥狀況下的開關門動作，搭上這樣的公車更是安心加倍。



想知道公車何時進站，除了可從智慧站牌上獲取到站資訊，方便的Gt Go App更是你的隨身導遊，不只能方便查詢附近站牌位置、公車到站狀態、公車到站前提醒，還可進階查詢大眾運輸系統轉乘資訊、台鐵、高鐵班次資訊、路線規劃等，或是獲取旅遊達人推薦的在地景點與住宿資訊。


另一款逛街利器嬉街趣App，拉近消費者與店家的距離。逛街時，只要一機在手，打開App，就可閱覽3D地圖，瀏覽商圈全景與店家，按圖索驥，找到心儀的店家，帶著手機在商圈逛街時，還能不時收到主動推送的優惠資訊。方圓一公里內的嬉街趣配合商家，能夠透過GPS定位技術與Beacon技術，把最新優惠好康或限時優惠主動推播到你的手機裡，讓你好吃好玩好拿，全不錯過，增添逛街的樂趣。便捷的交通運輸搭配智慧手機App，改變智慧城市的生活樣貌，所有輕鬆便利的服務都能藉由4G大寬頻與手機一手掌握，翻轉你我的生活。



Standing at bus stops with eyebrows frowning, looking at bus routes on the maps, such way of life is already outdated! The smart bus stops have been set by Taiwanese carriers at major roads in Taipei City. The large LCD screens clearly exhibit names of stops and instant information of buses, weather, news tickers as well as bus routes to make passengers easily acquire traffic information. During the waiting time, besides browsing the Internet for information via Gt 4G Wifi provided by smart stops, people can also view advertisement feed or replay related to nearby locations to get to known the neighboring recommended shops or discounts. The panorama surveillance cameras are set at the top of smart stops to record the scene around stops 24 hours not only to master the live action of vehicles, but also ensure the safety of passengers.


As the service quality of buses are getting promoted, the modern smart buses also joining the trend of 4G application to make the transportation service smarter and considerate. Besides the necessary free Gt 4G Wifi for passengers to play Pokemon Go, the in-vehicle devices can send back instant locations of vehicles to help mastering the status and driving routes, further ensuring the traffic safety. The smart bus also does not spare the bad drivers that constantly make emergency brake, acceleration or slowing down. The 4G smart bus can precisely record drivers’ behaviors, detect the opening-closing of bus doors, which makes passengers feel safer to take the vehicles.



If you want to know when the bus may arrive at the stop you are currently at, you can not only acquire traffic information from smart stops, convenient Gt Go App is also your personal guide. The nearby stops information, traffic feed, reminder of bus arriving and even advanced inquiry of MRT system transportation like Taiwan High Speed Rail and Taiwan Railways, routes planning or local tourist spots and hotel information recommended by traveling experts.


Another App called CJ App is a great tool for consumers to get closer to shops. When shopping, you can browse the 3D map of the App in your smartphone for the panoramic scene of shopping district and shops to get to the locations you desire. You can also randomly receive feeds of discount information while shopping on streets. If they are within the range of 1 km radius from your location, the member shops of CJ App can feed the latest discount or limited bonus information to your smartphone via GPS and Beacon techniques. You can apply these information to get the delicious and fun stuff and will not miss them. With convenient traffic transportation along with smartphone App to change the lifestyle of smart city, all convenient services can all be mastered by 4G wireless broadband and the smartphone in hands. Our life will be changed forever.
