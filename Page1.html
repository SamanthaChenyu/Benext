智慧城市

科技進駐古都 ， 打造智慧適居城市

Creating Smart Livable City by Implementing Technology into the Ancient Capital



漫步古都臺南，這座城市歷經三百多年歷史的淬煉與沉澱，發展出另一種獨特人文底蘊，穿梭在巷弄間品味老屋書店、食堂、咖啡館和藝文空間，處處都是驚喜。在地人對自己的故鄉充滿自信，從容的市井生活步調連結網路新世代科技，新舊交融出另一種智慧城市風貌。來古城散步，旅人只要「走」入其中，帶著一支手機，就能輕鬆掌握所有的生活脈動。


你對城市的未來有什麼想像呢，走一趟臺南就會發現4G寬頻與城市共同演進，默默地改變我們的生活，創造無限可能。從交通工具開始，隨時能掌握公車進站、路線查詢等資訊，可以更自在品嘗美食小吃或逛遊景點，不用擔心錯過班次。更可善用大臺南公車系統暢遊，依照GPS所在位置提供最佳搭乘建議，滿足市民通勤與旅行需求。在站牌、候車亭前也不用到處搜尋標示文字，只要拿起手機掃瞄行動條碼，就能知道該站相關的公車資訊，還有貼心的雙語服務！


美好校園是外地遊人羨慕的另一種學習環境，走在圍牆外，傳來的不再是唸課文的聲音，而是學生合力栽種農作、觀察自然生態的笑聲！遠傳電信打造了一個專為中小學師生設計的戶外教育行動學習平台「雲遊學」，改變了教室的樣貌。教育不只侷限於課室內，輔以行動科技，讓行動學習變成新潮流。影音教學社群讓服務能普及到城市與鄉村，不僅能為課程留下生動的記錄，也縮短各學區的城鄉差距。


近年來路跑活動盛行，有許多人乘著晚風用另一種速度感受古都樣貌，市府衛生局貼心提醒大家要注意保暖，推行「遠距健康照護智慧寬頻應用城市計畫」，讓民眾養成量血壓的健康自主管理。有別於以往傳統血壓機，每次量測的血壓數據，可透過手機下載Smart健康APP，立即查看和追蹤數字，4G雲端血壓機幫你顧健康，結合社區醫療群的資源提升市民就醫品質與成效，是一大福祉。


在全球氣候變遷的影響下，防災應變的重要性不容小覷，遠傳電信利用4G寬頻應用在水利即時防災，防汛第一線實際使用者穿戴高科技設備，能更快、更清晰回傳各地即時影像，零時差掌握災害現場狀況，讓救援工作更確實。臺南市幅員遼闊，發展行動式水文資訊監控平台，可強化智慧城市發展基礎，也保障人民的生活安全。一波波數位發展促進城市的變革，上網、滑手機已漸為智慧生活的一部分，以人為核心的智慧城市已經來了！



Strolling down the streets of Tainan, the Ancient Capital that saturated with the history of 300 years, which developed into a unique kind of humanity background, you can find surprise at every corner of old houses, book stores, canteens, café, and art and culture centers by passing though alleys. The locals are confident with their hometowns, the easy urban life pace connects the technology of Internet generation, another form of smart city with olds and news is constructed. Every traveler can easily master the life hood of this City with a mobile phone at his/her hands when walking in the Ancient Capital.


What is your imagination for the future of cities? Come to Tainan and you will find the co-evolution of 4G broadband and the city that silently change our way of life and create infinite possibilities. Starting from traffic vehicles, the entry and route search for buses can be accessed anytime, moreover, allowing you to enjoy delicacies and snacks and travel around tourist spots, not to worry about missing buses. The Tainan Bus System can be accessed for providing best bus proposals via GPS locations to satisfy the needs of commuting and traveling of citizens. No signs of words are required to be displayed at bus stops, instead, all you need is pick up you mobile phones and scan the barcodes, and the related bus information of such stop will be acquired, and with consideration, the bilingual service is provided!


The campus is another great learning environment for the non-locals, what you heard crossing out from fences will no longer be students reciting the contents of textbooks, instead, the laughter from students who work together on growing crops and observe the nature! Far EasTone has established “Cloudplay for Education” especially for junior high and elementary school students’ outdoor activities and change the context of classrooms. The classroom range will not be limited within the range of the room, with the assistance of mobile technology, the mobile education will be the new trend. The video education community can spread such service from urban areas to countryside, leaving not only vivid records but also reducing the urban-rural gap of each school district. By recording students’ progress, parents, teachers and students can trace their learning footprints and develop more possibilities of deep learning.


Nowadays, the long-distance running activities are becoming more popular, many people attempt to enjoy the scenery of the Ancient Capital. At the same time, Department of Health, Tainan City Government reminds considerately that people should be aware of keeping warm and promote the long-distance health care service to have people get used to having health management such as measuring blood pressure. Being different from conventional sphygmomanometers, the produced e data of blood pressure for each measurement can be accessed or tracked via the downloaded Smart Health App from mobile phones. Combining with the raised effects of citizens’ hospitalizing quality of community health care resource, the 4G cloud sphygmomanometer can take good care of you.


Under the influence of global climate change, it is crucial to focus on the disaster prevention practice. Far EasTone’s 4G is applied to the instant flooding disaster prevention, the field workers of flood control wear the high-tech devices to send back the most timely and clearest image at every locations for the authority to comprehend the disaster without time gap and make to rescue mission more practical. The range of Tainan is deep and wide, by developing the mobile hydraulic control platform, the infrastructure of smart city can be reinforced and secure the citizen lives. Wave after wave of digital development improving the city’s evolution, with the phenomenon of surfing the Internet and mobile phones for each person, you can see that the smart city of human factors is coming!
