整個雲端都是我的圖書館

Smart Mobile Reading to Make the Cloud to be My Own Library



走進大高雄，整齊的城市街道規畫，便捷的交通路網，新舊並蓄與多元族群融合，加上在地前衛的藝術氛圍，打造出南台灣獨樹一幟的城市風格。高雄人不只享有城市榮光，串連行動4G與雲端大數據，以數位科技加值與應用，市民得以享受更便利、更幸福的生活體驗，成就智慧城市典範。


不管你是正在高速運行的高鐵上，行駛中的捷運車廂，或是窩在家中的沙發，只要打開智慧型手機，輕鬆點兩下，就可把全臺14個城市，上萬本嶄新電子書借回家。臺灣雲端書庫與高雄市立圖書館合作，提供上萬本最新電子書籍與雜誌，供讀者免費借閱，讀者每借出一本，市府就會回饋12元給出版者、創作者與書庫平台，讓閱讀正向循環，回饋到智慧知識的創作者身上，協助文創永續經營，真正落實「公共出借權」（Public Lending Right），更加健全台灣的數位閱讀環境。


與一般圖書館電子書借閱不同，公共市立圖書館有電子版權限制，熱門搶手書籍不見得隨點隨借，讀者可能得等候14天或30天，有借閱使用的限制。台灣雲端書庫的最大好處，是可讓上百萬人同一時間閱讀同一本書，免出門、免等待、免排隊，操作簡便，沒有時空限制，一鍵登入就可瀏覽近400家出版社，上萬本電子書，還有熱騰騰的當期雜誌，滿足讀者對知識的渴望。



臺灣雲端書庫把圖書館變大，跨越地理限制，銀髮族或不易接觸圖書館的讀者，都能輕易使用這套服務。2016年，臺灣雲端書庫達1300萬次，遠從美國、歐洲等147個國家的讀者都能跨海借閱，真正讓閱讀做到無遠弗屆。


臺灣雲端書庫也配合高雄市政府「行動圖書館」，進入偏鄉推廣閱讀文化，把書香散播到每一個角落。當載著「臺灣雲端書庫Touch!」與上千本紙本書的智慧行動書車駛入三埤國小時，透過虛擬與現實的結合，即使偏鄉的孩子也能感染閱讀的魅力。「臺灣雲端書庫Touch!」的大螢幕上，顯現大數據抓取的Top5書籍，形成漂亮漂浮的書封星河，吸引孩子渴望的目光。


伸出手指滑動六角形知識魔方，立即可閱覽書籍介紹、出版社資訊、推薦資訊以及借閱QR Code，喜歡的書掃一下，立刻入袋，享受智慧走讀的便利，城鄉差距默默消於無形。透過智慧閱讀建構智慧城市，讓每個人都能享有智慧知識的價值，城市因網路科技而有了更多不同風貌。



The neatly planned urban streets, convenient traffic network, the old and new integration with the multi - ethnic groups, coupled with the cutting-edge artistic atmosphere, creating a unique style of the Southern Taiwan city. Kaohsiung citizens not only enjoy the glory of the city, the series of mobile 4G and cloud big data are utilized by digital technology value added approach, the public can enjoy more convenient and happier life experience to achieve of the model of smart city.


Whether you are on the fast-running high-speed rail, in the moving MRT, or nest in the home couch, you can easily borrow tens of thousands of new electronic books from all 14 cities in Taiwan by turning on the smart phone and make few clicks. Taiwan's cloud library and Kaohsiung City Library have worked together to provide the latest tens of thousands of electronic books and magazines for readers to borrow for free. For each book the readers borrow, the City Government would give back 12 NTD to the publishers, authors and library platforms to make positive reading circulation and feedback to the creators of intellectual knowledge, helping the the growth of cultural creative sustainable management. Thus, the implementation of the “Public Lending Right” would be reinforced, and Taiwan's digital reading environment would be sounder.


Unlike the conventional library e-book borrowing, the public city library has electronic copyrights restrictions. The popular books are not necessarily fetched and borrowed, the readers may have to wait 14 days or 30 days, there are restrictions on the use of borrowing. The best advantage of Taiwan's cloud library is allowing millions of people simultaneously read the same book which is free from the stepping out, waiting, lining, and easy to operate. There is no time limit. One key login can browse nearly 400 publishers, tens of thousands of e-books, as well as the hot current magazines to meet the reader's desire for knowledge.



Taiwan's cloud library can make it easier to use the library by making the library larger and beyond geographical limitation. The elderly or those who cannot access to the library easily can all utilize such service. In 2016, Taiwan's cloud library had reached 13 million times of downloads, readers from the United States, Europe and other 147 countries can log on to borrow, which make reading to be as far as possible.


Taiwan's cloud library also cooperates with the Kaohsiung City Government’s "mobile library" and goes to the rural areas to promote reading culture to spread the books to every corner. When the mobile librarian trucks carrying the "Taiwan Cloud Library Touch!" and thousands of paper books to San-pi Elementary School, by combination of virtual and reality, even children in rural areas can be attracted by the charm of reading. On the big screen of “Taiwan's cloud library Touch!" that shows the Top5 books captured by big data, the formation of beautiful floating book Galaxy has attracted children’s desiring eyes.


You can read the book introduction, press information, recommended information and borrowing QR Code by sliding the hexagonal magic cube of knowledge, you may get your favorite books with a scanning of QR code, and enjoy the convenience of smart mobile reading, the urban and rural gap is silently disappearing in the invisible path. By using smart mobile reading to construct the smart city, everyone can enjoy the value of intellectual knowledge, the city, thanks to Internet technology, can have more different styles.
