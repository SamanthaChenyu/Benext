數位串流直播影音平台 融入在地生活圈

igital Streaming Live Video Platform that integrated into Local Lifestyle



在全球行動4G網絡更加普及化的潮流下，數位串流的影音服務與應用已經開始深入到每一個家庭中。隨著智慧行動寬頻的網速不斷，台灣大哥大的智慧影音服務，讓民眾隨時隨地，不管身在何處，只要打開手機、平板或電腦，就可把手機螢幕上的myVideo影音隨看畫面投射至家庭劇院大螢幕，讓家中客廳變身成為頂級劇院；搭公車、坐捷運的空檔，也能離線觀看租用影片，打發通勤時間。戴上耳機，在公園一邊慢跑，一邊聽著台灣酷樂時代myMusic推薦歌單，時時刻刻享受高品質的影音放送。


受惠於大數據精準的演算模式，台灣大得以參考用戶行為，歸納出用戶喜愛下載的圖鈴風格，偏好歌曲與愛看的影片類型，主動推薦好歌清單或精選影片。不管愛看的是驚悚、歡樂、幼兒、古典、電音或搖滾，主動推薦的介面讓用戶使用上更貼心。

除了透過4G高速頻寬優勢，創造民眾有感的智慧影音新體驗，台灣大哥大也善用my Video與myMusic的核心服務，與縣市政府合作「在地影音專區」，點開myMusic App，在地影音專區提供與當地文化特色或推廣觀光結合的影音作品或微電影，讓縣市政府的地方建設特色融入在地生活圈，更加貼近民眾的閱聽體驗。


有好點子、好創意，卻苦無平台發表？台灣大「智慧影音計畫」每年提供不同影音徵選，提供的在地素人文創發表平台。2016年的「myfone行動創作獎」，特別徵選台灣在地縮時攝影創作徵件，讓閱聽民眾能瀏覽到不同的台灣風光，也讓有心創作者的作品有露出的機會，培植台灣文創產業，打造成為兼具在地特色與華人文創輔導的影音互動開放平台。



智慧影音計畫不只提供素人舞台，更有計畫地打造未來明日之星。遇到具備潛力的歌手，台灣大願意挹注資金協助素人歌手拍攝MV、拍攝形象廣告，安排演唱會或電視節目通告，協助素人解決後續行銷推廣作品的難題。在全民直播超夯的時代，台灣大也陸續完成台北、台南、高雄與花蓮等地的跨年演唱會直播，演唱會開播前數個小時，透過APP推播、在地簡訊（LBS）與社群操作，用戶能更快擷取到演唱會資訊與直播時段，不錯過精彩的播放時刻。


4G應用提供高速寬頻的暢快服務，讓民眾更能輕鬆享受便利、即時的影音娛樂，改變我們的生活型態，為下一波智慧城市打造美好的未來。



Under the trend of popularization of global mobile 4G net, the digital streaming video services and applications have been introduced to each household. As the speed of smart wireless broadband is getting promoted, people can access to smart video service of Taiwan Mobile anytime and anywhere via smartphones, tablets or computers to project the video of myVideo to the big screen of home cinema, making the living room become top class theater. People can also watch rent videos offline on the public transportations to consume the commuting time, or you can listen to songs from the recommended playlist of myMusic while wearing earphones during jogging in the park to enjoy high quality of video feed any time.


Being benefited from the precise calculation pattern of big data, Taiwan Mobile is able to take reference from users’ behaviors and further to conclude the ringtone styles favored by users or preferable songs and films types to actively recommend playlists or selected movies. No matter the users like thrillers, comedies, children, classical, electronic or rock n roll, the proactive recommending interface will always make users feel very considerate.


Besides using the advantages of 4G high speed broadband to create the fresh experience of smart video for people, Taiwan Mobile also utilizes the core service of my Video and myMusic and cooperates with local governments to introduce “Local Videos” services. By clicking myMusic App, the section of Local Videos provide films, short films or audio works related to local features and promote the tourism. By doing so, it enables local governments to integrate their local construction features into local social circles to get closer to the audio experiences of local people.


You have good ideas and innovations but lack of platform to present? The “Smart Video Project” of Taiwan Mobile provides different competitions of video types each year for local ordinary creators to present their creation. The “Mobile Innovation Award” in 2016 especially organized a competition to select the time-lapse photographs of Taiwan locations. It invites audiences to browse different beautiful sceneries of the Island while allowing dedicated artists to have the chance to exhibit their works, and further fostering Taiwanese cultural and creative industry. By doing so, it enables the creation of interactive platform combining local features and Chinese culture and creative guidance.



The project is not just for ordinary persons, it is also equipped with intentions to foster the future stars. Taiwan Mobile is willing to provide financial assistance to potential ordinary singers to shoot music videos, image advertisements, arrange the concerts or TV program invitations and help the ordinaries to solve artworks promotion and sales. Under the crazy trends of live streaming, Taiwan Mobile has also gradually completed the live steaming of New Year's Eve concerts in Taipei, Tainan, Kaohsiung and Hualien. The live streaming is fed on APP, LBS or communities few hours before the concerts. The users can get concert information and live streaming schedules faster and will not miss any precious moment.


The 4G application provides fast services of high speed broadband that enables people to enjoy convenient and instant video entertainment. It has changed our life style and built a brighter future for the next trend of smart cities.
